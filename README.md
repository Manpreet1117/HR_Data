# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard

## About This Project

Ever wondered why employees leave a company — and whether 
it's possible to predict who's at risk? I built this HR 
Analytics Dashboard in Power BI to analyze employee 
attrition patterns, workforce demographics, and performance 
trends across departments.

## Questions I Set Out to Answer

- What is the overall attrition rate and which departments 
  are most affected?
- Does age group or education field influence attrition?
- How does overtime impact employee performance ratings?
- What does the salary distribution look like across roles?
- Which job roles have the highest turnover?

## What I Found

- Attrition varies significantly across age groups and 
  education fields — younger employees and certain 
  education backgrounds show higher exit rates
- Employees working overtime show notably different 
  performance patterns compared to those who don't
- Salary (monthly income) and years at company are key 
  factors that correlate with attrition risk
- Certain job roles consistently show higher headcount 
  loss than others

## Tools I Used

- Power BI for dashboard design and interactive visuals
- DAX for calculated measures (Attrition Rate, Avg Salary)
- Dataset: HR Analytics dataset (IBM/Kaggle)

## Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards: Attrition count, Attrition Rate, 
  Avg Salary, Employee Count, Avg Age, Working Years
- Attrition by Education Field (Donut Chart)
- Attrition by Age Group (Bar Chart)
- Performance Rating by Department & Overtime (Column Chart)
- Attrition and Job Role distribution by Age (Bar Chart)
- Interactive Slicers for Department and Job Role filtering
